1.Enable Firewall rules logging.
2. Click "Create Export" at the top of the "Logs Exports" page.
3. Fill in the "Edit Export" section as follows.
4. Enter the "Sink name".
5- Select "BigQuery" as the "Sink Service".
6. In "Sink Destination" select  "BigQuery", Select or create the particular dataset to receive the exported logs. You also have the option to use partitioned tables.
7. Click "Update Sink" to create the sink.
